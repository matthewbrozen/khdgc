Come out and shoot a round with us!

We are a PDGA Affiliated Club

We are a group of people who love disc golf and are locals of Kenneth Hahn Disc Golf Course. We have weekly events on Wednesday evenings and are planning more events for the weekend. Most days there are people playing at the course, introduce yourselves and grow the community. We will be organizing more events and park cleanups throughout the summer and hope to grow the community.

Weekly Doubles Meetup Wednesday Evening

Starting June 21st 2021 there will be weekly doubles every Wednesday at 5pm. Look for Matt at hole 1. Check in on U-Disc at 5pm, start at 5:15pm. We will flip for doubles partners and then there will be prizes for the winning team and the losing team. Then after the round stick around for a CTP. We are planning to offer a package deal in September for a ongoing league with cash prizes so keep an eye out. Please wear a mask and stay 6ft+ apart. Stay safe and have fun.
