Club Details

If you are a new player looking to find a group to learn from or a experienced player looking for some competition all are welcome. Kenneth Hanh is free Monday through Friday with ample parking all around the course. On the weekends it costs $8 dollars to enter the park. Currently there are people meeting Wednesday and Friday at 1pm informally as we find the best times. We will be having online meetings once a month to talk about the course until the pandemic is over to plan how to better organize for the club. Looking for people to get involved in multiple project related to the course and expanding the sport in general.

Starting March 14th 2021 there will be weekly doubles every Sunday at 8 am. Look for Roch or Jackson at hole 1. Please wear a mask and stay 6ft+ apart. Stay safe and have fun.
