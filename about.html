Club Details

If you are a new player looking to find a group to learn from or a experienced player looking for some competition all are welcome. Kenneth Hanh is free Monday through Friday with ample parking all around the course and costs $7 dollars to enter on the weekend. You can park at the Stonebrook Nature Preserve and walk to the course for free during the weekends or you can enter from Jim Gilliam Park to avoid paying to enter during the weekend. Currently Matt is at the course during the week Monday through Friday around 12pm and he has plently of extra discs if you want to come try the sport for the first time. We have two weekly events a singles on Wednesday at 5pm and a dobules event Sundays at 8am. We are hoping to organize regular clean ups and events. If you have any ideas feel free to reach out to Matt.
